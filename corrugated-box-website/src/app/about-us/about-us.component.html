About Us

Krishna Packaging is a Fully Automatic Packaging Company established in the year 2002 in Bangalore. We provide total packaging solutions under one roof with exclusive design & safety, and our products are always appreciated in the market for superior quality and durability.

Our organization has a good reputation in the market based on our reliability, excellence & pro-activeness. Because of the exceptional quality and longevity, our products are well-liked in the market. With a direct connection to the consumers, our team operates in a highly responsible manner. We have all the modern types of equipment and machinery handled by the best resources in the business, supervised by the keen eyes of our leadership.

We have a dedicated, efficient & well-experienced team for production & quality assurance to help us meet our client’s expectations in terms of product quality & delivery timelines. Our team works round the clock in a responsible manner and will always be easily approachable to our customers.

Our market standing is built on our dependability, excellence, and proactive nature. We are supplying an extensive variety of our products to many industrial sectors such as Automobile, Pharmaceutical, Electronics, Electrical, Garments, Food & Beverages, etc. We are aware that a successful business model is always a nice blend of having sophisticated machinery with the best people & a loyal customer base. Hence, we have inculcated a customer-first attitude within our team, and each one of us believes in developing long-term relationships with our customers. Meeting deadlines & customer satisfaction have always been our top priority, due to which we have a long list of happy & satisfied customers with us.
